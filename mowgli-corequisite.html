What Makes Everyday Tasks Possible

In each example, you are given prompt sentence, such as:

Net is used for catching fish, only if

or

Net is used for catching fish unless

and you are asked to complete the prompt sentence with the first reasonable thing that comes to your mind which either makes the prompt sentence possible, such as:

Net is used for catching fish, only if we are in the water

or makes the tasks impossible to happened, like:

Net is used for catching fish, unless the net has a large tearing


Please note that, our goal is to capture the most common perceptions that comes to your mind and is specific to each prompt sentence, so make sure your answers are specific, concise and usual. Below, we have compiled a short list of common bad responses:

  1. Avoid Pronouns for all the things referred inside the prompt sentence. For instance, in the examples below, as "the net" and "fish" are mentioned in the sentence you must not use a pronouns to refer to them. The only exception for using pronoun is for referring the person(s) performing the act (fishing in this case) where using pronouns is preferred.
    The net is used for catching fish, unless
    • it has large tearings.
    • the net has large tearings.
    • we are in a desert.

    The net is used for catching fish only if
    • There are some fish in the water.
    • we are in the water.
  2. Do Not Overthink: Avoid unusual responses that require a lot of backstory. We are looking for things that are obvious to you but not mentioned in the sentence.
      The net is used for catching fish, unless
    • the boat is sinking.
    • there is an earthquake.
  3. Be Specific: Avoid general responses that are not specific to the prompt sentence. Especially, please avoid responses that can be used for any similar sentence.
      The net is used for catching fish, unless
    • the boat is broken.
    • the net is broken.
    • the net is not working.
  4. Avoid Negatives: Avoid writing using negatives (e.g. not) in your responses.
      The net is used for catching fish, only if
    • the net does not have a large tearing.
