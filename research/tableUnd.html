Tabular Data to Knowledge Graph Matching
Highlights:
  • -> Aim of this research is to understand tabular data based on actual contents and the meta-data of table (e.g. styling, headers) and map the content to entities and relations from knowledge graphs such as dbpedia and wikidata
  • -> This work was prepared for ISWC (2019) table understanding challenges
Publications:
  1. Thawani A, Hu M, Hu E, Zafar H, Divvala NT, Singh A, Qasemi E, Szekely PA, Pujara J. Entity Linking to Knowledge Graphs to Infer Column Types and Properties. InSemTab@ ISWC 2019 (pp. 25-32).
